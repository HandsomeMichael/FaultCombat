Stamina points
- player have stamina points to use on skills or dodgeroll

Dodgeroll
- player can initiate dodgeroll
- player can get "perfect dodgeroll" on 4 frame precise dodge
- perfect dodgeroll grant few buffs
- dodge accesories reworked to instead amplify dodgeroll


EGO resources
- player can have ego resources
- critical hit builds up a stack of resources based on damage type
- regular hit maintains ego resources
- if player hasnt hit anything in 2 seconds, deplete ego resources

Ego List :

Wrath > Melee
Gloom > Ranger
Pride > Magic
Sloth > Summoner
Envoy > Rogue
Limbo > Generic / Accesories

Each 100 damage dealt, transform into one ego resource


Skill attack
- individual weapon can be applied with skill attack
- skill use ego resources, scaled by weapon rarity
- only 1 skill can be active at a time and each have 15 seconds cooldown
